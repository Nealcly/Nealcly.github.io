Leyang Cui （崔乐阳）

I am a senior researcher at Natural Language Processing Center, Tencent AI lab. I obtained my Ph.D. degree at Zhejiang University and Westlake University, advised by Prof. Yue Zhang. My research interests include sequence labeling, syntactic parsing, and chatbot.
